Sample Itinerary

Cuba (Thursday – Sunday)

  • Thursday: Arrive in Havana, check into hotel, group welcome dinner
  • Friday: Old Havana walking tour, classic car ride, salsa night
  • Saturday: Day trip to Viñales (tobacco farms, caves, horseback riding)
  • Sunday: Free morning, beach visit, flight home

Curacao (Thursday – Sunday)

  • Thursday: Arrive in Willemstad, check into oceanfront hotel, welcome drinks
  • Friday: Island tour (Handelskade, Queen Emma Bridge, Shete Boka Park)
  • Saturday: Snorkeling at Tugboat Beach, visit Playa Kenepa
  • Sunday: Leisure morning, shopping, return flight

Thailand (Thursday – Sunday)

  • Thursday: Arrive in Bangkok, check-in, welcome dinner with Thai cuisine
  • Friday: Visit Grand Palace, boat ride on Chao Phraya River, explore Khao San Road
  • Saturday: Day trip to Ayutthaya or floating markets, Thai massage
  • Sunday: Street food breakfast, temple visit, airport transfer

Belize (Thursday – Sunday)

  • Thursday: Arrive in Belize City, transfer to island resort, sunset dinner
  • Friday: Snorkeling in Hol Chan Marine Reserve, visit Shark Ray Alley
  • Saturday: Explore Mayan Ruins or take a jungle adventure
  • Sunday: Breakfast by the beach, check-out, flight home
